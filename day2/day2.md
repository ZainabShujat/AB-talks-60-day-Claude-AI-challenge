# Day 2 - Prompt Engineering

## Overview

Today I learned about **Prompt Engineering**, one of the most important skills when working with AI tools such as Claude and ChatGPT.

Prompt Engineering is the practice of writing clear and structured instructions so that AI models can better understand the task and generate more useful outputs.

The main idea is simple:

> Better prompts lead to better responses.

---

## What I Learned

### What is Prompt Engineering?

Prompt Engineering is the process of designing effective prompts that help AI understand:

* What task needs to be completed
* Who the audience is
* What format the response should follow
* How detailed the output should be

Instead of giving vague instructions, prompt engineering focuses on providing context and clarity.

### Why It Matters

AI models are powerful, but they depend heavily on the quality of the instructions they receive.

A well-written prompt can:

* Improve response accuracy
* Save time by reducing revisions
* Generate more structured and useful outputs

---

## Weak vs Improved Prompt

### Weak Prompt

```text
Write about dogs.
```

### Improved Prompt

```text
You are a veterinarian. Write a beginner-friendly guide for new dog owners. Include three essential dog-care tips and explain each in simple language.
```

### Observation

The improved prompt provides:

* A role
* A target audience
* A specific task
* Output requirements
* Desired writing style

As a result, the AI generates a much better response.

---

## Three Practical Benefits of Better Prompts

1. More accurate responses
2. Reduced back-and-forth corrections
3. Better performance on complex tasks
