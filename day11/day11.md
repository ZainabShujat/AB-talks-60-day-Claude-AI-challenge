## Key Learnings

1. **ATS systems are keyword-first** — a strong resume that doesn't use the exact terms recruiters search for (like "MERN stack", "OOP", "DSA") gets filtered out before a human ever sees it.
2. **GATE qualification is underutilized** — for SDE roles, it's a strong signal of CS fundamentals and should be in the summary, not buried at the bottom.
3. **Rephrasing vs. inventing** — ATS optimization is about surfacing what you've already done using the language the job description uses. No fabrication needed.
4. **CodeChef rating hints at DSA** — but ATS won't infer that. Explicitly mentioning "Data Structures & Algorithms" and "competitive programming" makes it scannable.
5. **Project descriptions need architecture keywords** — mentioning OOP, scalability, and system design decisions in project bullets dramatically improves SDE role alignment.
