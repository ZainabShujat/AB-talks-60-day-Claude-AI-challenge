🫁 DETAILED AIR IMPACT
🫁

Lungs

PM2.5 at 62 µg/m³ (17× WHO limit) penetrates deep alveoli. Chronic exposure causes bronchitis, reduced lung capacity, and increased COPD risk. Children and elderly face acute inflammation.

😴

Sleep

High particulate matter disrupts respiratory passages, causing snoring, mouth-breathing, and reduced REM sleep. Oxidative stress elevates cortisol, fragmenting sleep cycles.

⚡

Energy Levels

Continuous exposure to pollutants increases inflammation and mitochondrial stress, causing chronic fatigue. Oxygen delivery efficiency drops measurably at AQI 150+.

🏃

Exercise Performance

Outdoor exercise at AQI 152 increases pollutant inhalation 6× compared to rest. VO2 max decreases. Morning hours (7–9 AM) are particularly dangerous — AQI peaks then.

💧 DETAILED WATER IMPACT
💇

Hair Fall & Dryness

Ghaziabad's water has high TDS, heavy metals (lead, chromium from industrial runoff), and hard-water minerals. These strip the hair's natural lipid barrier, causing breakage and thinning.

🔬

Scalp Health

Chlorine and heavy minerals disrupt scalp microbiome. Combined with PM2.5 settling on the scalp, this causes dandruff, folliculitis, and blocked follicles leading to hair loss.

🧴

Skin Dryness & Acne

Hard water leaves calcium/magnesium deposits that disrupt skin's acid mantle (pH 4.5–5.5). This weakens the moisture barrier and creates a breeding ground for acne-causing bacteria.

🌸

Sensitive Skin

Airborne PM2.5 deposits on skin trigger inflammatory cytokines. Long-term exposure accelerates skin ageing, exacerbates eczema, and worsens rosacea for sensitive skin types.

✅ Personalized Recommendations
☀️ DAILY ACTIONS
😷

Always wear N95/KN95 outdoors

Surgical masks filter only 60% of PM2.5. N95 filters 95%. Non-negotiable at AQI 152+.

🕐

Time outdoor activities carefully

AQI is lowest around 4–6 PM in Ghaziabad. Avoid 7–9 AM peak pollution window.

🪟

Keep windows closed during peak hours

7–10 AM and 6–9 PM are high-traffic, high-pollution windows. Seal gaps around doors.

💧

Hydrate with filtered water — 3L/day

Helps kidneys flush heavy metals and toxins absorbed from polluted air and water.

🏠 INDOOR AIR IMPROVEMENTS
💨

HEPA Air Purifier (CADR 300+)

Run in bedroom 24/7. Prioritize brands with activated carbon filter to also catch VOCs and NO2.

🌿

Air-purifying plants

Spider plant, peace lily, and snake plant reduce indoor PM2.5 and VOCs. Place near windows.

🧹

Vacuum with HEPA filter

Regular vacuuming prevents PM10 from resettling. Wipe surfaces with damp cloth daily.

🕯️

Avoid burning incense/candles

Indoor combustion sources generate significant PM2.5 in enclosed spaces — worsening already poor indoor air.

🏃 OUTDOOR ACTIVITY GUIDANCE
🕔

Best window: 4 PM – 6 PM

AQI in Ghaziabad drops to its daily minimum in late afternoon. Safest window for walks, cycling, or light exercise.

🚫

Avoid 7 AM – 10 AM outdoors

Morning traffic + overnight pollutant accumulation creates daily AQI peaks. CPCB data shows Ghaziabad averages 161 AQI at 6 AM — its worst hour.

😷

N95 mandatory for all outdoor activity

At AQI 152+, even brief outdoor exposure without a mask increases inhaled PM2.5. For runs or cycling, use a sports-fit N95 with exhalation valve.

🏋️

Move intense workouts indoors

During exercise, breathing rate increases 6×, meaning 6× more pollutant intake. Gyms with HEPA filtration are strongly preferred over outdoor exercise.

🌧️

Post-rain window is safest

Rain washes PM2.5 and PM10 from the air. AQI can drop 30–50% in hours after rainfall — monitor the AQI app and use that window for outdoor time.

💇 HAIR CARE RECOMMENDATIONS
🚿

Install shower filter (hard water)

Vitamin C or KDF shower filters remove chlorine and reduce heavy metals in bath water — critical for Ghaziabad's hard water.

🧴

Chelating shampoo weekly

Removes mineral buildup from hard water. Use once/week to prevent brittle, dull hair.

🛡️

Anti-pollution hair serum

Apply before going outdoors. Creates a barrier against PM2.5 settling on scalp and strands.

💆

Scalp oil massage (weekly)

Coconut or argan oil massages stimulate follicles and counteract the drying effect of hard water and air pollutants.

🧴 SKIN CARE RECOMMENDATIONS
🌅

Antioxidant serum (Vitamin C)

Neutralizes free radicals from PM2.5 exposure. Apply before SPF every morning — your most important anti-pollution step.

☀️

SPF 50+ sunscreen daily

Pollution + UV = accelerated skin ageing. SPF is non-negotiable even on cloudy days in Ghaziabad.

🧼

Double-cleanse at night

Oil cleanser first removes PM2.5 particles, then a gentle cleanser clears residue. Never sleep with pollution on skin.

🛁

Barrier moisturizer morning & night

Ceramide-based moisturizers rebuild the skin barrier compromised by hard water and air pollutants. Apply while skin is still slightly damp.

💧 WATER QUALITY IMPROVEMENTS
🪣

RO + UV filter for drinking water

Removes heavy metals, chlorine, and biological contaminants. Target TDS of 50–150 ppm after filtering for safe drinking water.

🧪

Test your water TDS quarterly

Ghaziabad groundwater TDS commonly exceeds 500 ppm. A ₹200 TDS meter helps you verify your filter is working effectively.

🍋

Add a lemon squeeze to hard water

Citric acid binds calcium and magnesium ions in hard water, reducing scale and making the water gentler on hair and skin if used for rinsing.

🔧

Replace filter cartridges on schedule

Expired RO membranes can leach stored contaminants back into water. Replace every 6–12 months as per manufacturer guidelines.
